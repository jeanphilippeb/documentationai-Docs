## Overview

Jules provides a comprehensive set of tools to manage the entire commodity trading lifecycle. You create and track operations, manage contracts and pricing, coordinate logistics, handle invoicing, and monitor margins — all from a single platform. These features help trading teams replace fragmented spreadsheets and email chains with structured, connected workflows.

<Callout kind="info">
Jules is designed for progressive adoption. Start with operations and contracts, then activate logistics, invoicing, and margin modules as your workflows require.
</Callout>

## Trade Lifecycle Management

Every trade in Jules is represented by an **operation** — the central entity that connects commercial terms, logistics, and financials. Operations follow a structured lifecycle with built-in approval workflows.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Operation" icon="plus-circle">
    Create from a contract or directly. Define counterparty, qualities, quantities, and pricing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit for Approval" icon="check-circle">
    Route the operation through your organization's approval chain with role-based permissions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Load Containers" icon="package">
    Add containers with weights, qualities, and loading details — or manage bulk stockpiles for local trades.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Book & Ship" icon="anchor">
    Create freight bookings, assign shipping lines, and track shipments to destination.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invoice & Settle" icon="dollar-sign">
    Generate invoices from container lines, reconcile payments, and close the trade.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
Operations support two market modes: **Export** (international containerized shipments) and **Local** (domestic bulk deliveries). The mode determines which logistics flows and margin calculations apply.
</Callout>

## Contracts and Pricing

Define commercial agreements that prefill operations automatically. Contracts capture counterparty terms, quality streams, price formulas, and delivery schedules — reducing manual data entry and ensuring consistency.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Fixed Pricing" icon="lock">
    Set a fixed price per metric ton at contract creation. Ideal for spot deals and short-term agreements.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Index-Based Pricing" icon="bar-chart-2">
    Link prices to market indices (LME, TSI, Platts) with premium/discount adjustments. Prices update automatically based on quotation periods.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Formula Pricing" icon="percent">
    Build custom formulas combining indices, fixed components, and percentage-based adjustments for complex pricing structures.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

| Feature | Description | Use Case |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| Quality Streams | Multiple material grades per contract | Mixed-quality shipments |
| Price Suggestions | AI-assisted pricing from market data | Quick offer creation |
| Offers & Portal | Share commercial offers via external portal | Counterparty collaboration |
| Allocation Matching | Link purchase and sale operations | Back-to-back trade tracking |

## Margin Analytics

Track profitability in real time across every level of your trading portfolio. Jules calculates margins automatically from operation data, freight costs, hedging positions, and unexpected costs.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Container Margin" icon="box">
    Per-container P&L combining purchase price, sale price, freight, insurance, and hedging gains/losses.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Operation Margin" icon="activity">
    Aggregated margin across all containers in an operation, including unexpected costs and budget variances.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Dashboard Margin" icon="pie-chart">
    Portfolio-level analytics with filters by period, counterparty, commodity, and market mode.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

| Metric | Calculation | Visibility |
|--------|-------------|------------|
| Gross Margin | Sale revenue − Purchase cost | Per container |
| Net Margin | Gross − Freight − Insurance − Unexpected costs | Per operation |
| Hedging P&L | Hedging gain/loss allocated to containers | Per container |
| Budget Variance | Actual margin vs. planned budget | Per operation |

<Callout kind="info">
Margins update automatically as containers are loaded, invoiced, and paid — giving you a live view of profitability without manual reconciliation.
</Callout>
