---
title: Adding Docs to Your Project
description: Quick guide for adding documentation in MineHub.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your project">
    Click on your project from the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a new doc">
    Select the "Docs" tab and click "Add Document".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write and save">
    Add your content and press "Save" to finish.
  </Step>
</Steps>
