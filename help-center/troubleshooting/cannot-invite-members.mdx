---
title: Can't Invite Members
description: Fix issues with team invitations.
---

## Issue

- "Invite" button is disabled
- Members not receiving invites

## Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check project role">
    Only owners and admins can invite members to a project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify email addresses">
    Make sure emails are correct and not already linked to other teams.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check spam folder">
    Sometimes invite emails go to spam. Ask recipients to check there.
  </Step>
</Steps>
